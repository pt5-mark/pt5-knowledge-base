### 🛠️ 10套可直接复制的Prompt模板（附适用场景）
门派一：时光穿梭者（怀旧/历史向）

**8-bit像素风**

提示词：8-bit pixelated art style, NES game aesthetic, vibrant blocky colors, jagged edges, retro digital atmosphere

适用场景：红白机游戏解说、童年回忆类内容

**胶片漏光风**

提示词：Vintage Polaroid photo style, film grain, light leaks, muted tones, soft focus, 1990s aesthetic, nostalgic vibe

适用场景：老相册感内容、年代故事讲述

门派二：脑洞大开系（创意/治愈向）

**软萌3D风**

提示词：Super cute 3D style, soft pastel colors, rounded shapes, chibi character design, warm lighting, joyful atmosphere, clay texture

适用场景：儿童科普、治愈系内容

**达利怪诞风**

提示词：Super weird surrealist art, dreamlike logic, melting objects in the style of Salvador Dali, abstract composition, vibrant and odd colors

适用场景：梦境/潜意识主题、哲学话题

**定格黏土风**

提示词：Stop-motion clay animation style, plasticine texture, handmade look, fingerprint details, soft studio lighting, Aardman animation vibe

适用场景：手工质感内容、小红书爆款风格

门派三：视觉轰炸系（营销/科技向）

**波普艺术风**

提示词：Pop Art style in the style of Andy Warhol, high contrast, bold outlines, Ben-Day dots, bright neon colors, comic book aesthetic

适用场景：潮流营销、吸睛解说

**赛博朋克故障风**

提示词：Digital art in the style of Cyberpunk 2077, neon lights, glitch effects, futuristic city interface, dark background with glowing cyan lines

适用场景：科技主题、AI/未来感内容

门派四：稳健不出错系（科普/商务向）

**国家地理大片风**

提示词：Hyper realistic 4K photography, highly detailed, cinematic lighting, sharp focus, national geographic documentary style

适用场景：自然科普、专业纪录片

**极简手绘风**

提示词：Hand-drawn illustration style, clean lines, flat colors, modern editorial art, minimalist and easy to understand

适用场景：复杂概念解释、知识科普

**瑞士国际设计风**

提示词：Swiss graphic design style, international typographic style, grid layout, sans-serif typography, bold geometric shapes, high contrast, minimalist

适用场景：数据可视化、商务演示

### 💡 关键技巧

善用“in the style of”指令：直接引用艺术家/作品风格（如Van Gogh、Cyberpunk 2077），调用AI的艺术知识库

- 复制粘贴即可使用：无需英语基础，模板适配NotebookLM自定义视频功能
